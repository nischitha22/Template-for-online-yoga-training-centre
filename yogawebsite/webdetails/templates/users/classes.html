HOT YOGA

8:30AM - 9:00AM

Hot yoga is a vigorous form of yoga performed in a very warm and humid studio. There are many different types of hot yoga classes. During the Bikram form of hot yoga, the room is heated to approximately 105 F (40 C) and has a humidity of 40 percent.


MEDITATION YOGA

9:30AM - 10:00AM

Yoga meditation combines the benefits of yoga's physical exercises with the positive energy of meditation practice. It's also one of the most accessible meditations for many people to do. Yoga meditation may help you achieve better mental health and overall wellbeing.
